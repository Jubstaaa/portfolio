I'm İlker — a software engineer based in Istanbul. I build production web apps with **TypeScript end-to-end**: mostly React and Next.js on the frontend, Node.js or Hono on the backend, with PostgreSQL or MongoDB behind them.

Most of my work sits at the intersection of fast UIs and real-time systems. Some of what I've shipped lately:

- **Hono Telescope** — a Laravel-Telescope–style debugger for Hono apps on Bun & Node.js. Open source.
- **Prosbase** — a League of Legends esports stats platform with a real-time ingest pipeline.
- A handful of smaller things: a collaborative pixel-art canvas with WebSockets, browser-based puzzle games, automation bots, and a Unity-based side project or two.

Before going deep on my own projects I spent time at agencies and startups in Istanbul — shipping wallets, corporate portals, internal tools — across a pretty wide stack. I pick up tooling fast: I've shipped **React Native/Expo** mobile apps, **Electron** desktop tools, and **Unity** games when the job called for it. I'm comfortable owning infra too — **Nginx + Ubuntu** VPS, CI/CD with **GitHub Actions**, deploys on **Vercel**, **Cloudflare**, and **DigitalOcean**.

Currently finishing my **BSc in Software Engineering** at İstanbul Nişantaşı Üniversitesi.

Outside work: games (Cyberpunk 2077, Silent Hill 2 Remake lately), anime, and too many side-project ideas I'll definitely ship this time.
