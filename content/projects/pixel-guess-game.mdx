Pixel Guess Game is an engaging web-based application that challenges players to guess pixelated images from various categories, including **League of Legends**, **Valorant**, **Demon Slayer**, and more. Players must identify the character or item as quickly as possible before the image becomes fully visible. The game is designed to be both entertaining and competitive, featuring a streak system, a global leaderboard, and various difficulty levels.

## **How It Works: Gameplay Overview**

In **Pixel Guess Game**, the objective is simple: guess the character or item displayed in pixelated form before it becomes fully visible. Here’s how the game works:

- Players choose a category (e.g., League of Legends, Valorant, Demon Slayer).
- A pixelated image of a character from that category appears on the screen.
- The player has to guess which character it is before the pixelation decreases.
- If you guess incorrectly, more pixels are revealed until the full image is shown.
- Correct guesses reward you with streaks, and the longer your streak, the higher your rank on the leaderboard.

### **Multiple Difficulty Levels**

**Pixel Guess Game** offers different difficulty modes to challenge players:

- **Easy Mode**: The image is less pixelated, making it easier to guess.
- **Hard Mode**: The image starts with more pixelation, and a **grayscale filter** adds extra difficulty.

## **Leaderboard & Streak System**

One of the most exciting features of **Pixel Guess Game** is the global **Leaderboard**. As you play, your correct guesses will contribute to your streaks, and the longer your streak, the higher you’ll rank. Here’s how it works:

- **Track Your Streak**: Keep track of your longest streak of correct guesses.
- **Leaderboard**: Compare your scores with other players around the world to see where you stand.
- **Competitive Play**: Compete with friends or random players and strive to be the best.
