**Bishy** is a modern mobile wallet solution that enables users to manage personal and business transactions efficiently and securely. Designed with scalability and accessibility in mind, Bishy supports a full range of financial services—available in both Turkish and English. From cashback rewards to corporate virtual POS systems, the platform provides a flexible and responsive experience for different user types.

## **Homepage & Platform Overview**

The homepage presents a clear and modern layout that introduces users to the core services. It also offers access to other key informational pages, such as About Us, FAQ, Blog, and Contact.

- Supports both **light and dark themes**
- Clean layout with easy navigation
- Language toggle for Turkish & English

## **Bishy Individual Features**

The **Bireysel (Individual)** module allows users to:

- Track expenses and cashback rewards
- Manage their **Bishy Cards**
- View **Fees & Limits** with full transparency
- Perform daily transactions securely

## **Customer Panel**

The web-based **customer panel** mirrors many features of the mobile app, allowing users to:

- Manage their accounts
- Access transaction history
- Contact support and handle financial operations online

This ensures usability even for users without direct app access.

---

## **Admin Dashboard**

The internal **Admin Dashboard** is used by the team to:

- Manage website content (e.g., blog posts, FAQ updates)
- Monitor customer reports and feedback
- Generate and export system usage reports
- Oversee user data and behavior (via Firebase)

You handle CI/CD for this panel via **GitHub Actions** on **Linux**, ensuring robust development workflows and quick deployment cycles.

---

## **Corporate Dashboard**

This dashboard supports business clients with:

- Virtual POS setup and transaction tracking
- Payment links for customer billing
- Analytics on payment behaviors

It’s tailored for B2B clients, offering data clarity and control.

---

## **Call Center & Communication Management**

Customer communications, support queries, and operational logistics are managed internally through connected systems. The dashboard also includes tools for:

- Creating tickets
- Monitoring call center performance
- Handling customer feedback

## **Deployment & Infrastructure**

You're managing the infrastructure using:

- **Linux-based server environments**
- **CI/CD via GitHub Actions**
- Automated testing & deployment pipelines for each panel

This ensures high availability and faster iteration cycles.
