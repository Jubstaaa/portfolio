## How It Works: Gameplay Overview

In Emoji Guess Game, your goal is simple:

Guess the word, title, or character represented by a series of emojis. Here’s how the game works:

- Choose a Category: Select from categories like Movies, TV Series, Books, Games, Anime, Songs, and LoL Champions.
- Emoji Puzzle Appears: A set of emojis representing a word, title, or character is displayed.
- Type Your Guess: Enter your answer in the input fields before skipping or moving to the next puzzle.
- Skip Feature: Stuck? Use the skip button to get a new random puzzle instantly.
- Track Your Progress: See how many puzzles remain unsolved in each category.
