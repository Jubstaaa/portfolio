## 1. **Attack on Titan**

A gripping blend of action and mystery, _Attack on Titan_ kept me on the edge of my seat. Its philosophical themes and unexpected plot twists added depth to the storyline, making it a binge-worthy experience that I completed in just a week.​

---

![Neon Genesis Evangelion](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/neon-genesis-evangelion.webp)

## 2. **Neon Genesis Evangelion**

Though it doesn't have the rapid pacing of some modern series, _Neon Genesis Evangelion_ offers a thought-provoking journey. Its exploration of psychological and sociological themes, combined with religious symbolism, creates a unique atmosphere that resonates even decades after its release.​

---

![Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/demon-slayer-kimetsu-no-yaiba.webp)

## 3. **Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba**

With its stunning animation and compelling storyline, _Demon Slayer_ stands out as a modern masterpiece. The battles are visually spectacular, and the protagonist's determination to grow stronger adds emotional weight to the narrative.​

---

![Cyberpunk: Edgerunners](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/cyberpunk-edgerunners.webp)

## 4. **Cyberpunk: Edgerunners**

Set in the vibrant world of Night City, _Cyberpunk: Edgerunners_ delivers a concise yet impactful story. The series masterfully combines a melancholic narrative with top-tier animation, making it a memorable watch even for those unfamiliar with the _Cyberpunk 2077_ game.​

---

![Kuroko's Basketball](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/kuroko-s-basketball.webp)

## 5. **Kuroko's Basketball**

As a fan of sports anime, _Kuroko's Basketball_ hit all the right notes. The intense matches, character development, and team dynamics reminded me of classics like _Captain Tsubasa_, making it a thrilling ride from start to finish.

---

![Code Geass](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/code-geass.webp)

## 6. **Code Geass**

_Code Geass_ offers a gripping tale of strategy and rebellion. The protagonist's tactical genius and the series' intricate plot twists kept me engaged throughout. Its emotional depth and thought-provoking themes make it a standout in the mecha genre.​

---

![Death Note](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/death-note.webp)

## 7. **Death Note**

A classic battle of wits, _Death Note_ remains one of my all-time favorites. The psychological duel between Light and L is both thrilling and intellectually stimulating. I've revisited the series multiple times, always captivated by its intricate storytelling.​

---

![Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/yu-gi-oh-duel-monsters.webp)

## 8. **Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters**

Rewatching _Yu-Gi-Oh!_ as an adult brought a wave of nostalgia. Beyond the card games, the series offers engaging storylines and character arcs that stand the test of time. It's a delightful blend of childhood memories and timeless entertainment.​

---

![Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/anohana-the-flower-we-saw-that-day.webp)

## 9. **Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day**

Seeking an emotional narrative, _Anohana_ delivered a heartfelt story of friendship and loss. Its concise storytelling and poignant moments left a lasting impression, making it a touching experience despite its brevity.​

---

![Naruto](/images/blog/anime-series-i-have-enjoyed-over-the-years/naruto.webp)

## 10. **Naruto**

The anime that sparked my journey into the anime world, _Naruto_ holds a special place in my heart. From its diverse cast of characters to the intricate world-building, the series offers a rich tapestry of stories and emotions. Witnessing the characters' growth and their individual journeys made it an unforgettable adventure.​

---

Each of these series has offered unique experiences, enriching my appreciation for the diverse world of anime. Whether you're new to anime or a seasoned viewer, I hope this list provides inspiration for your next watch.​
